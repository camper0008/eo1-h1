math used:

constants

G[kg-1 * m3 * s-2] = 6.674 * 10-11 
M[kg] = "earth mass" = 5.9722 * 1024 kg
R[m] = "earth radius" = 6.3781 * 106 m
r = R + "orbit height"

velocity

v2 = G * M * (1 / r)
v = sqrt(v2)

circumference

c = 2 * PI * r

units

v2 [m2/s2] = G[kg-1 * m3 * s-2] * M[kg] * r^-1[m-1]
[m2/s2] = [kg-1 * m3 * s-2] * [kg] * [m-1]
m2/s2 = kg-1 * m3 * s-2 * kg1 * m-1
m2/s2 = kg1 * kg-1 * m3 * m-1 * s-2
m2/s2 = kg0 * m2* s-2
m2/s2 = m2 * s-2

source: Orbital Maths at NASA with Chris Hadfield (Stand-up Maths)
